Growth/Inflation Outlook : Improving

Growth :
  • FY24 (P): India likely to emerge Fastest Growing Economy for third consecutive year
    • Driven by cyclical (demand) and structural factors (reforms).
    • Aided by robust govt capex and resilient consumption.
  • FY25 (P) Resilient Growth.
    • Limited impact likely from global spillovers & tight monetary policy
  • Risk to View :
    • Geo-political conflicts, global recession and loss of momentum.
  • FY24 inflation eased to 5.4%-5.5% range (FY23 : 6.7% y/y) - with core likely ~4.5%
  • FY25 (P) inflation expected to moderate further to 5% mark
    • Base effect (food), normal monsoon, growth moderation, fiscal consolidation and positive real rates.
  • Risk to View : Volatile food prices, high crude and commodity prices.
Source : CEIC, CSO, RBI, NIMF Research

Current Monetary Policy : From Rate hike to Liquidity Management

  • Current Cycle Effective rate hike :315 bps FY24 (till Dec 2023)
  • RBI’s line of Action (Action & expectations)
    • Long Pause to continue. (started in April 2023)
    • High Forex Intervention & Tight Liquidity Management
      • Substitute for policy rate hike
    • OMOs (Open Market Operations) optionality remains open
  • FY25 : RBI’s action likely to be impacted by :
    • Resilient growth and gradual decline in inflation
    • Expect rate action in 2H (in line with US Fed action).
